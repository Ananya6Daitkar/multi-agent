# FutureBoard 2-Minute Video Script

## 0:00-0:15 Hook

Companies have dashboards for the past, but leaders still discover the consequences of major decisions only after making them. FutureBoard gives enterprises an immune system for the future.

## 0:15-0:35 Problem

AcmeCorp is a B2B SaaS company considering a risky decision: replace 30% of customer support with AI within 90 days to save 12 million dollars per year. Finance may like the savings, but the board also needs to understand churn, VIP trust, employee morale, legal exposure, rollout risk, and competitor response.

## 0:35-1:05 Solution Demo

FutureBoard opens a Band-powered multi-agent decision room. Strategy, Finance, Customer, Employee, Operations, Red-Team, Competitor, Legal Risk, Negotiator, and Memory agents collaborate through the room. They publish structured context, hand off work, challenge assumptions, raise disagreement, trigger escalation, negotiate a safer plan, and store decision memory.

## 1:05-1:25 Technology

Band is the collaboration layer. It coordinates room state, handoffs, events, escalation, and memory. Groq or AI/ML-compatible inference powers high-reasoning agents like Strategy, Finance, Customer, and Negotiator. Featherless AI powers specialist stress-test agents such as Employee, Competitor, and Red-Team when configured.

## 1:25-1:45 Outcome

The original plan is flagged as high risk. The agents disagree, the Red-Team finds failure paths, and Legal opens escalation. The Negotiator proposes a safer plan: automate repeat tier-one volume first, protect VIP and regulated accounts with human-in-loop routing, redeploy support staff into AI QA and escalation, and review metrics at 30, 60, and 90 days.

## 1:45-2:00 Closing

FutureBoard is not a chatbot for executives. It is an enterprise decision immune system. Companies should not discover the consequences of a decision only after making it.
